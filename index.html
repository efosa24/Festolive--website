Process

How an engagement typically works.

A clear, transparent process so you know what to expect — and when you’ll start seeing impact.

1
Discovery & alignment
Clarify goals, KPIs, data sources, and decision-makers. We agree on success metrics and timelines.
2
Data audit & design
Review current data flows, quality issues, and reporting. Propose data models and dashboard wireframes.
3
Build & iterate
Implement data pipelines, dashboards, and models with iterative feedback from stakeholders.
4
Launch & enable
Deploy to production, train users, and hand over documentation and playbooks.

Typical timeline: 4–8 weeks from kickoff to first go-live, depending on scope and data complexity.

For many clients, we start with a 4–6 week “pilot” project: one high-impact dashboard or forecasting model, fully built and automated. From there, we expand into a roadmap that your team can own and grow.
